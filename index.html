Our Mission

Preparing for Uncertainty

Infantry commanders operate in environments defined by incomplete information and rapidly changing conditions.
Our mission is to train & prepare commanders for the moment when planning collapses and leadership decisions determine the outcome.

“War is the realm of uncertainty; three quarters of the factors on which action in war is based are wrapped in a fog of greater or lesser uncertainty” — Carl von Clausewitz

Capabilities

The Fog Of War Command Simulation

The Simulation

Commander-perspective training

Commanders train in a first-person tactical simulation, making decisions from the same perspective they face in combat as scenarios evolve in real time.

Decision-Making Under Pressure

Assess. Decide. Act.

Adaptive audio-visual stimuli increase stress and cognitive load, forcing commanders to prioritize, issue orders, and adapt under pressure.

The Thinking Enemy

An opposition that reacts

Enemy forces model real hostile behavior, flanking, withdrawing, and applying pressure in response to commander actions

Debrief & Decision Analysis

Facts over memory

Every session is recorded and reviewed with synchronized replay and structured analysis, providing personalized feedback on tactical performance

Deployment

Zero Infrastructure. Maximum Reach

Stride integrates into existing infantry training frameworks with minimal personnel and logistical overhead.

Laptop-Based, Off-the-Shelf

Runs on standard laptops, with no dedicated facilities or bespoke hardware required.

Commander Training Only

Training targets battalion commanders across ranks, with no line soldiers needed

Training as a Service

Scales from Individual to Battalion

Training scales from individual commanders to full battalion-level exercises

About Us

Built from the Ground Up

For infantry, by infantry.

Stride Interactive is built by a team of infantry special forces veterans and commanders across the ranks , from junior leaders to senior officers with extensive operational and training experience.

The team has trained, led, and evaluated hundreds of commanders across units and operational contexts, with firsthand experience of how training decisions translate into performance under fire.

That experience shapes our approach to combat preparation, grounded in real-world constraints, friction, and decisions made under pressure.

The battlefield is evolving.
Your training should too.
