Our Mission

War is the realm of uncertainty

Infantry commanders operate in environments defined by incomplete information and rapidly changing conditions.
Our mission is to train & prepare commanders for the moment when planning collapses and leadership decisions determine the outcome.

"War is the realm of uncertainty; three quarters of the factors on which action in war is based are wrapped in a fog of greater or lesser uncertainty"
— Carl von Clausewitz

Capabilities

The Fog Of War Command Simulation

01

The Simulation

Command training from the commander's perspective.

Commanders train inside a first-person tactical simulation that places them at the center of the fight. They observe, command, and decide from the same perspective they will face in combat, while scenarios evolve in real time and plans immediately collide with reality.

02

The Command Loop

Assess. Decide. Act. Under pressure.

Advanced audio-visual stimuli introduce progressive stress and cognitive load as pressure increases. Commanders are forced to prioritize, issue orders, and adapt under mental fatigue and uncertainty, strengthening decision-making over time.

03

The Thinking Enemy

Opposition that reacts, adapts, and exploits mistakes.

Enemy forces are modeled on real hostile behavior. They flank, withdraw, apply pressure, and respond dynamically to commander actions, ensuring no two sessions unfold the same way.

04

Debrief & Decision Analysis

Objective truth over subjective memory.

Every session is recorded and reviewed through synchronized replay and structured analysis. Commanders receive clear feedback on decision-making, timing, and command effectiveness, replacing subjective memory with observable truth.

Deployment

Zero Infrastructure. Maximum Reach

Stride integrates into existing infantry training frameworks with minimal personnel and logistical overhead.

Laptop-Based, Off-the-Shelf

Stride runs as software on standard laptops, with no dedicated facilities, bespoke hardware, or fixed installations required.

Training as a Service

Delivered as a managed training capability, with setup and logistics handled on-site so units can focus on training.

Minimal Personnel Requirement

Training sessions require only the commanders being trained and a small instructional presence.

Scales from Individual to Battalion

Training can be conducted for individual commanders, small teams, or full multi-commander battalion-level exercises.

About

Born from the Ground Up

For infantry, by infantry.

Stride Interactive is built by a team of infantry special forces veterans and commanders across the ranks — from junior leaders to senior officers with extensive operational and training experience.

The team has trained, led, and evaluated hundreds of commanders across units and operational contexts, with firsthand experience of how training decisions translate into performance under fire.

That experience shapes our approach to combat preparation, grounded in real-world constraints, friction, and decisions made under pressure.
